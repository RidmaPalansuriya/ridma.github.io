My Signature Project

EnergyEffix

EnergyEffix is an innovative electricity management mobile application designed to help users monitor and reduce their energy consumption. By connecting to a customized IoT Smart Meter, it provides real-time data on electricity usage, allowing users to set personalized reduction goals. The app leverages machine learning algorithms to offer predictive insights and actionable tips, making energy management both efficient and user-friendly. EnergyEffix is a testament to our commitment to sustainability and smart technology, empowering users to take control of their energy usage and contribute to a greener future.
